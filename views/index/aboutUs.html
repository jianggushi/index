OUR TEAM

At present, the research and development team of Fanhan Tech is made up of 10 developers (including 2 doctors and 8 masters). This energetic and young team is responsible for the development of auxiliary tools for data acquisition and data processing, such as speeches, pictures, texts, videos, etc. Fanhan Tech also has 6 professional project management members. They are experienced in project management, quality control and cost optimization. They can meet the personalized needs of data collection, processing and data annotation, and can achieve the goal of high efficiency, low cost and high quality data delivery.
